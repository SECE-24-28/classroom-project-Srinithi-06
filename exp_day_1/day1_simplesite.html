Campus and Infrastructure

The campus is designed to provide a conducive and positive learning environment, spanning approximately 20.54 acres. Academic Facilities: The college features smart classrooms, high-tech labs (including for AI/ML, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, etc.), and a fully equipped recording studio for faculty and students. It leverages Google Classroom and Moodle LMS for hybrid learning models. The library provides access to NPTEL resources. Hostels & Transport: Hostel facilities offer a secure, comfortable, and Wi-Fi-enabled stay with amenities like a gym, laundry, and dining options. Bus transport is also provided for day scholars from surrounding areas. Health & Wellness: A fully functional medical center with a full-time doctor and nurses handles emergency health services. The college also conducts student wellness programs, including yoga sessions.

ABOUT US

Sri Eshwar College of Engineering (SECE) is a private, autonomous institution established in 2008 near Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, affiliated with Anna University. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs and is recognized for its focus on outcome-based education, strong research facilities, industry linkages, and modern infrastructure. The college holds accreditations from NBA and NAAC and has received awards like the AICTE-CII Award for Industry Linked Emerging Institute
